FAQ

Corporate Voice Over Questions

Can you match our existing corporate voice?

Yes. Send us a sample of your current voice over, and we'll match tone, pace, and delivery style. We can also provide multiple voice matches if you're looking to establish a new brand voice.

Do you offer rush service?

Yes, we offer 12-hour rush delivery for urgent corporate projects. Additional rush fees apply. Contact us for availability.

What if I need updates to my IVR later?

We maintain your IVR scripts and can record new prompts anytime. Our IVR packages include 6 months of free updates. After that, we offer discounted rates for additional prompts.

Do you offer multi-language corporate services?

Yes, we offer corporate voice overs in Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Japanese, and 20+ other languages. Native speakers with professional broadcast experience.

Can you handle sensitive or confidential content?

Absolutely. We sign NDAs and handle all corporate content with strict confidentiality. Many of our clients trust us with their most sensitive internal communications and pre-release information.

What file formats do you deliver for IVR?

We deliver IVR-optimized files in WAV, MP3, or any format required by your phone system. Common formats include: 8kHz/16-bit mono, 16kHz, and 22kHz. We can match your system specifications exactly.
