FAQ

Commercial Voice Over Questions

How long does a commercial voice over take?

Most commercial spots are delivered within 24 hours. Rush service available for 12-hour turnaround. The actual recording session is quick - most 30-60 second spots are recorded in under 30 minutes, with editing completed shortly after.

Do you offer broadcast rights?

Yes, all our commercial packages include broadcast rights. Our Professional and Premium packages include full national broadcast rights. Additional buyout options for unlimited usage are available.

Can I direct the session remotely?

Absolutely! We offer live remote direction via Source-Connect, Zoom, or phone. You can guide the performance in real-time from anywhere in the world. This is included in our Professional and Premium packages.

What if I need revisions?

We include free revisions with every package. Basic includes 1 revision, Professional includes 2 revisions, and Premium includes unlimited revisions. All revisions are completed within 12-24 hours.

Can I hear different voices before choosing?

Yes! We'll provide 2-3 voice matches based on your script and requirements. You can hear custom auditions before committing to a full recording. This service is complimentary.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver broadcast-ready WAV files (48kHz/24-bit) and MP3s. We can also provide specific formats required by your production team or advertising agency.
