    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions

    How long does a typical voice over project take?

    Most commercial and corporate projects are completed within 24-48 hours. Longer projects like audiobooks typically take 1-3 weeks depending on length. Rush service is available for urgent needs.

    What file formats do you deliver?

    We deliver in any format you need: WAV, MP3, AIFF, FLAC. Standard delivery is 48kHz/24-bit broadcast-quality WAV files with MP3 versions included.

    Can I direct the session remotely?

    Absolutely! We offer live remote direction via Source-Connect, Zoom, or phone. You can guide the performance in real-time from anywhere in the world.

    What if I need revisions?

    We include free revisions with every project. Number of revisions varies by package, but we work with you until you're completely satisfied.
