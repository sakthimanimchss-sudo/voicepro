FAQ

Studio Questions

Can I bring my own engineer?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own engineer. Studio rate remains the same. Please let us know in advance so we can prepare workspace.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48+ hours before session: full refund. Within 48 hours: 50% cancellation fee. Within 24 hours: full session charge.

Do you offer after-hours sessions?

Yes, we offer 24/7 availability for an additional $25/hour surcharge. Advance booking required.

Can I test my Source-Connect connection before booking?

Absolutely! Contact us to schedule a free 15-minute test session. We'll verify your connection quality and audio settings.

What file formats do you deliver?

Standard delivery: 48kHz/24-bit WAV. We can provide any sample rate (44.1k, 48k, 96k), bit depth (16, 24), or format (WAV, AIFF, MP3, FLAC) upon request.

Is parking available?

Yes, free dedicated parking is available behind the building. We'll provide parking instructions upon booking confirmation.
