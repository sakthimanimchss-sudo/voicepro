FAQ

Character Voice Questions

How do you create a custom character voice?

We start with your character description - personality, age, background, and vocal qualities you imagine. Our actor provides 3-5 voice approaches, we refine the direction, and develop the final character voice. This process typically takes 24-48 hours.

Can one actor do multiple characters in the same project?

Yes! Many of our actors can perform 3-5 distinct character voices in a single project. We ensure each character sounds unique and consistent throughout. Perfect for indie games and small productions.

Do you offer remote direction sessions?

Absolutely. Live remote direction via Source-Connect, Zoom, or your preferred platform is included with most character packages. You can direct the performance in real-time from anywhere in the world.

What if I need voice matching for an existing character?

We specialize in voice matching. Send us a sample of the existing voice, and we'll match tone, pacing, and delivery. Perfect for continuing a character series or replacing an actor.

Do you provide raw files or edited dialogue?

All dialogue is professionally edited and mastered. We deliver clean, noise-free files with consistent levels, properly trimmed and named according to your specifications.

What rights do I get for character voices?

All character packages include full usage rights for your project. Buyout options for unlimited use across all media are available. Character voices are exclusive to your project - we won't reuse them.
