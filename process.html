01
Step One

Script Submission & Project Brief

Tell us about your project

Every great voice over starts with a clear understanding of your project. Submit your script along with details about your target audience, desired tone, and project goals.

What to include:

  • Script in .txt, .doc, or .pdf format
  • Project type (commercial, narration, etc.)
  • Desired voice characteristics (age, gender, tone)
  • Target audience and demographics
  • Deadline requirements
  • Budget range

Timeline:

  • Initial review: Within 1 hour
  • Quote delivery: Within 4 hours
  • Project confirmation: Same day
Pro Tip: Include pronunciation guides for industry terminology, brand names, or unusual words. This ensures accuracy and saves revision time.
Secure script upload portal
02
Step Two

Voice Selection & Talent Matching

Find the perfect voice for your project

Based on your project brief, we select 2-3 voice talent matches from our roster of professional voice actors. Listen to custom auditions and choose the voice that best represents your brand.

Voice matching criteria:

  • Vocal quality and timbre
  • Age and character appropriateness
  • Accent and dialect requirements
  • Industry experience
  • Previous client reviews
  • Availability and turnaround

Timeline:

  • Voice recommendations: 2-4 hours
  • Custom auditions: 4-6 hours
  • Selection window: 24 hours
Pro Tip: Not sure which voice to choose? We provide audio samples and can guide you based on your target audience and industry best practices.
Listen to custom auditions
03
Step Three

Professional Recording Session

Studio-quality recording with optional live direction

Your selected voice talent records your script in our broadcast-quality studio. Every session is engineered for optimal audio quality using industry-standard equipment and acoustic treatment.

Recording specifications:

  • Neumann U87 or equivalent microphone
  • Universal Audio Apollo interface
  • Acoustically treated booth
  • 48kHz/24-bit broadcast quality
  • Source-Connect available
  • Live remote direction

Timeline:

  • Recording session: 30-60 minutes
  • Raw file delivery: Immediate
  • Live direction available: Real-time
Pro Tip: Join the session live via Source-Connect or Zoom to provide real-time direction. This ensures the performance matches your vision exactly and reduces revision needs.
Live direction available
04
Step Four

Editing, Mastering & Quality Control

Precision audio engineering for broadcast-ready sound

Our audio engineers edit and master your recording to perfection. We remove mouth clicks, normalize levels, reduce noise, and ensure consistent audio quality across all files.

Post-production process:

  • Comping best takes
  • Noise reduction and cleanup
  • Level normalization
  • Breath control
  • EQ and compression
  • Export in requested formats

Timeline:

  • First draft delivery: 24-48 hours
  • Revision turnaround: 12-24 hours
  • Final mastering: 2-4 hours
Pro Tip: Need specific file formats or naming conventions? Provide your specifications before we begin editing to ensure seamless integration with your production workflow.
Precision audio engineering
05
Step Five

Revisions & Client Feedback

Your satisfaction is guaranteed

We include free revisions with every project. Listen to your first draft and request changes - whether it's a different inflection, pacing adjustment, or pronunciation correction.

Revision policy:

  • Commercial: 1-2 rounds included
  • Narration: 2-3 rounds included
  • Corporate: 2 rounds included
  • Character: 2-3 rounds included
  • Additional revisions: $75/session

Timeline:

  • Revision requests: Within 5 business days
  • Revision delivery: 12-24 hours
  • Final approval: Client sign-off
Pro Tip: Be specific with revision requests. Include time stamps and clear descriptions of desired changes. Example: "At 0:23, please emphasize 'limited time' more enthusiastically."
Unlimited revisions until satisfaction
06
Step Six

Final Delivery & Ongoing Support

Your files, delivered your way

Receive your final mastered audio files in any format you need. We provide secure download links, cloud storage, and ongoing support for your project.

Delivery options:

  • Formats: WAV, MP3, AIFF, FLAC
  • Sample rates: 44.1kHz, 48kHz, 96kHz
  • Bit depth: 16-bit, 24-bit
  • Delivery method: Download link, cloud storage
  • File naming: Custom naming conventions
  • Metadata: ID3 tags, chapter markers

Support:

  • File storage: 90 days
  • Re-downloads: Unlimited
  • Technical support: Lifetime
  • Future updates: Discounted rates
Pro Tip: Save your project confirmation number for easy access to your files. We maintain secure backups for 90 days after delivery.

FAQ

Process Questions

How long does the entire process take?

Most commercial and corporate projects are completed within 24-48 hours from script submission to final delivery. Narration and audiobooks take longer based on length. We provide estimated delivery dates with every quote.

Can I make changes after hearing the first recording?

Absolutely! We include free revisions with every project. Simply provide your feedback and we'll make the adjustments. Most revisions are completed within 24 hours.

What if I don't like the voice I selected?

If you're not satisfied with the initial recording, we offer one complimentary re-record with a different voice talent. This ensures you get the perfect voice for your project.

Do you offer live direction sessions?

Yes! Live remote direction via Source-Connect, Zoom, or phone is available for all project types. This is included with our Professional and Premium packages. Standard packages can add live direction for an additional fee.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in any format you need: WAV, MP3, AIFF, FLAC. Standard delivery is 48kHz/24-bit WAV files. We also provide custom file naming, metadata, and chapter markers upon request.

How do I submit my script?

You can upload your script directly through our contact form, email it to studio@voicemasterpro.com, or paste it into the project brief. We accept .txt, .doc, .docx, and .pdf formats.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before scheduled recording receive a full refund minus 10% processing fee. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the deposit but can apply it to a future session within 90 days.

Do you offer rush service?

Yes, rush service (12-hour turnaround for commercials, 24-hour for other projects) is available for a 50% rush fee. Subject to talent availability.
