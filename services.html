FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our voice over services

How do I choose the right voice style?

We'll help you select the perfect voice based on your brand, target audience, and project goals. You can also browse our demo reels to hear different styles. Schedule a free consultation for personalized recommendations.

What is your turnaround time?

Most projects are delivered within 24-48 hours. Commercial spots as fast as 24 hours, longer narration projects typically 48-72 hours. Rush service available upon request.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we include free revisions until you're 100% satisfied. Number of revisions varies by service package. All revisions are completed within 24 hours.

What file formats do you provide?

We deliver in any format you need: WAV, MP3, AIFF, FLAC. Sample rates: 44.1kHz, 48kHz, 96kHz. Bit depths: 16-bit, 24-bit. Custom formats available.

Can I direct the session remotely?

Absolutely! We offer live remote direction via Source-Connect, Zoom, or phone. You can guide the performance in real-time from anywhere in the world.

Do you offer commercial rights?

Yes, all voice overs include full commercial usage rights. Additional broadcast or buyout options available. Contact us for specific licensing needs.
