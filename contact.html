FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 4 hours during business hours. Most quotes are provided within 24 hours.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to book studio time and begin work. The remaining balance is due upon delivery of final files.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and checks for corporate accounts.

Can I direct the session remotely?

Yes! We offer live remote direction via Source-Connect, Zoom, or phone. This is included with most project packages.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Cancellations made 48+ hours before scheduled recording receive a full refund. Within 48 hours, deposit is non-refundable but can be applied to a future session.

Do you offer rush service?

Yes, we offer 12-hour rush delivery for urgent projects. Additional rush fees apply. Contact us for availability.
