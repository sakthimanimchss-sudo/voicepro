    1. Introduction

    VoiceMaster Pro ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website or use our voice over services, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

    This policy applies to information we collect:

    • On this website (voicemasterpro.com).
    • In email, text, and other electronic messages between you and us.
    • Through your use of our voice over and audio production services.
    • When you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites and services.

    Please read this policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our website or services. By accessing or using this website or our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

    2. Information We Collect

    2.1 Information You Provide to Us

    We collect several types of information from and about users of our website and services, including:

    • Personal Information: Name, email address, postal address, phone number, company name, and job title.
    • Payment Information: Credit card numbers, billing addresses, and other financial information (processed securely through third-party payment processors).
    • Project Information: Scripts, creative briefs, project specifications, and audio files.
    • Account Information: Username, password, and account preferences.
    • Communications: Records of your correspondence with us, including emails, chat messages, and phone calls.

    2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

    When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

    • Usage Details: Pages viewed, time spent on pages, links clicked, search queries, and navigation paths.
    • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, and unique device identifiers.
    • Location Information: General geographic location based on IP address.
    • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies.

    2.3 Information from Third Parties

    We may receive information about you from third parties, including:

    • Payment processors (payment confirmation, not payment details).
    • Social media platforms when you interact with our content.
    • Business partners and referral sources.
    • Publicly available sources.

    3. How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

    3.1 Providing and Improving Services

    • To provide voice over and audio production services you request.
    • To process and complete transactions.
    • To communicate with you about your projects.
    • To provide customer support.
    • To improve our website and services.
    • To develop new features and offerings.

    3.2 Marketing and Communications

    • To send you promotional emails and newsletters (you may opt out at any time).
    • To display relevant advertising.
    • To conduct market research and analysis.
    • To personalize your experience on our website.

    3.3 Legal and Security

    • To protect our rights, property, and safety.
    • To comply with legal obligations.
    • To enforce our Terms of Service.
    • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security, or technical issues.

    3.4 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

    For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal information under the following legal bases:

    • Contract Performance: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you.
    • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure security.
    • Consent: For marketing communications and cookies.
    • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws.

    4. Information Sharing

    We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

    4.1 Service Providers

    We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

    • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal).
    • Email marketing and communication (Mailchimp, SendGrid).
    • Cloud storage and file hosting (AWS, Google Cloud).
    • Analytics (Google Analytics).
    • Customer relationship management (HubSpot, Salesforce).

    These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

    4.2 Voice Talent

    We share your scripts and project requirements with our voice talent and production staff solely for the purpose of completing your project. All talent and staff are bound by confidentiality agreements.

    4.3 Legal Requirements

    We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

    4.4 Business Transfers

    If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or use of your personal information.

    4.5 With Your Consent

    We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

    5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

    5.1 What Are Cookies

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your experience.

    5.2 Types of Cookies We Use

    Cookie Type Purpose Duration
    Essential Cookies Required for website functionality, security, and accessibility Session / Persistent
    Analytics Cookies Track site usage, page views, and user behavior (Google Analytics) Up to 2 years
    Functionality Cookies Remember your preferences and settings Up to 1 year
    Marketing Cookies Display relevant advertisements and track campaign performance Up to 90 days

    5.3 Your Cookie Choices

    You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

    • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.
    • Cookie Consent Tool: When you first visit our site, you can customize your cookie preferences.
    • Opt-Out Links:
      • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
      • Network Advertising Initiative: NAI Opt-out

    5.4 Do Not Track

    Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals. We continue to evaluate emerging standards in this area.

    6. Data Security

    We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

    6.1 Technical Safeguards

    • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
    • AES-256 encryption for stored sensitive data.
    • Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning.
    • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems.
    • Secure, encrypted backups.

    6.2 Organizational Safeguards

    • Strict access controls - only authorized personnel can access client data.
    • Regular security training for all employees.
    • Confidentiality agreements for all staff and contractors.
    • Annual security policy reviews.

    6.3 Payment Security

    We do not store credit card information on our servers. All payments are processed by PCI DSS compliant third-party payment processors (Stripe, PayPal).

    6.4 Breach Notification

    In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours of discovery, as required by applicable law.

    7. Data Retention

    We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

    7.1 Retention Periods

    • Client Account Information: Retained for as long as your account is active, plus 1 year after account closure.
    • Project Files: Retained for 90 days after project completion, then permanently deleted from active storage. Backups retained for up to 1 year.
    • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years to comply with tax and financial reporting requirements.
    • Communications: Retained for 3 years for customer service purposes.
    • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt out or unsubscribe.

    7.2 Data Deletion

    When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted from our systems. Paper records are shredded, and digital files are securely overwritten before deletion.

    8. Your Privacy Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

    8.1 Right to Access

    You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

    8.2 Right to Rectification

    You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

    8.3 Right to Deletion

    You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.

    8.4 Right to Restrict Processing

    You have the right to request that we limit how we use your personal information.

    8.5 Right to Data Portability

    You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

    8.6 Right to Object

    You have the right to object to our processing of your personal information for direct marketing purposes.

    8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

    If we rely on your consent to process your information, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.

    8.8 How to Exercise Your Rights

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@voicemasterpro.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

    9. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

    If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

    9.1 Data Controller

    VoiceMaster Pro is the data controller for your personal information. Our EU representative can be contacted at eu-representative@voicemasterpro.com.

    9.2 International Data Transfers

    Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have data protection laws different from those in your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

    • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.
    • Privacy Shield certification (for transfers to certified organizations).

    9.3 Right to Lodge a Complaint

    You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have violated your privacy rights.

    10. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

    If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

    10.1 Categories of Personal Information Collected

    In the past 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information:

    • Identifiers (name, email, address, IP address).
    • Commercial information (purchase history).
    • Internet activity (browsing history, site interactions).
    • Professional/employment information (company name, job title).

    10.2 Categories of Sources

    We collect personal information directly from you, automatically from your device, and from third-party service providers.

    10.3 Business Purpose for Collection

    We collect personal information to provide services, process transactions, communicate with you, improve our website, and for security purposes.

    10.4 No Sale of Personal Information

    We do not sell personal information to third parties. We have not sold personal information in the past 12 months.

    10.5 Right to Opt-Out

    You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information. As we do not sell information, there is no need to opt out.

    10.6 Right to Non-Discrimination

    We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

    10.7 Authorized Agent

    You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf. We may require proof of authorization and verification of your identity.

    11. Children's Privacy

    Our website and services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@voicemasterpro.com. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

    For children's content projects, we work with parents or legal guardians who provide all necessary permissions and scripts. We do not directly interact with child talent without parental consent.

    12. Third-Party Links

    Our website may contain links to third-party websites, plug-ins, and applications. Clicking on those links or enabling those connections may allow third parties to collect or share data about you. We do not control these third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit.

    12.1 Social Media Features

    Our website includes social media features, such as the Facebook Like button and Twitter share button. These features may collect your IP address and which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing the feature.

    13. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, and other factors. When we make material changes, we will:

    • Post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
    • Notify you via email (if you have an account with us).
    • Display a prominent notice on our website for at least 30 days.

    We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of our website and services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

    FAQ

    Privacy Questions

    Do you share my scripts or audio files?

    Your scripts and project files are confidential. We share them only with the voice talent and production staff directly involved in your project. All talent and staff are bound by confidentiality agreements. We do not share your content with any third parties without your explicit permission.

    How long do you keep my audio files?

    We retain your final audio files for 90 days after project completion. Raw recordings and session files are deleted after 30 days. You can request extensions or permanent deletion at any time. We maintain secure backups for up to 1 year for disaster recovery purposes.

    Do you use my project in your portfolio?

    We only use client projects in our portfolio with your explicit written consent. You can opt out at any time. Many clients choose to allow us to showcase their work, which helps us demonstrate our capabilities to potential clients. If you prefer to keep your project confidential, simply let us know.

    Is my payment information secure?

    Yes. We do not store credit card information on our servers. All payments are processed by PCI DSS compliant third-party processors (Stripe, PayPal). Your payment details are encrypted and tokenized. We only receive confirmation of payment, never your full financial information.

    How do I request deletion of my data?

    Send an email to privacy@voicemasterpro.com with "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line. Please include your full name and email address associated with your account. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days. Certain information may be retained as required by law.

    Do you use cookies for tracking?

    Yes, we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze site traffic, and for marketing purposes. When you first visit our site, you can customize your cookie preferences. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings at any time.

    Have Privacy Questions?

    Our Privacy Officer is ready to assist you with any concerns about your data.
