FAQ

Narration Voice Over Questions

What is "per finished hour" (PFH) pricing?

Per finished hour is the industry standard for audiobook narration. You pay for each hour of completed, edited, and mastered audio. A 10-hour audiobook costs 10 PFH rate. This includes all recording, editing, proofing, and revisions.

How long does an audiobook take?

A typical 10-hour audiobook takes 2-3 weeks from script approval to final delivery. This includes recording (approximately 2-3 hours per finished hour), editing, proofing, and corrections. Rush service available.

Do you offer multi-voice audiobooks?

Yes, we can produce audiobooks with multiple narrators, full casts, or dual narration. Pricing varies based on number of voices and complexity. Contact us for a custom quote.

What if I need pronunciation research?

We include pronunciation research for technical terms, foreign words, and proper nouns. For specialized content (medical, scientific), we can work with your subject matter experts or provide additional research services.

Do you handle audiobook distribution?

We deliver ACX/Findaway ready files that you can upload directly to distribution platforms. We don't handle distribution directly, but we can recommend preferred partners and ensure your files meet all technical requirements.

Can I hear a custom audition?

Absolutely. Send us 300-500 words from your manuscript, and we'll provide a custom audition from 2-3 narrators that match your project's style and tone. This service is complimentary for projects over 3 hours.
