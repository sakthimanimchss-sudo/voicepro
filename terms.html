2. Services

VoiceMaster Pro provides professional voice over and audio production services including but not limited to:

  • Commercial voice over for TV, radio, and digital advertising
  • Narration for documentaries, audiobooks, and e-learning
  • Corporate voice over for training videos, IVR systems, and presentations
  • Character voice over for animation, video games, and creative projects
  • Audio editing, mixing, and mastering
  • Studio recording and live direction sessions

We reserve the right to refuse service to any client for any lawful reason.

3. Quotes and Pricing

All quotes provided by VoiceMaster Pro are valid for 30 days from the date of issuance unless otherwise stated. Quotes are based on the project specifications provided by the client. Significant changes to project scope, length, or requirements may result in a revised quote.

Pricing is determined by factors including but not limited to:

  • Script length and complexity
  • Voice talent selection
  • Usage rights and licensing
  • Turnaround time
  • Additional services (music, SFX, translation, etc.)

4. Payment Terms

4.1 Deposits

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to book studio time and commence work on all projects. The deposit secures your project in our production schedule.

4.2 Final Payment

The remaining 50% balance is due upon delivery of final audio files. Files will be released upon receipt of full payment.

4.3 Accepted Payment Methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, bank transfers, and corporate checks. Checks must clear before final files are delivered.

4.4 Late Payments

Projects placed on hold due to non-payment are subject to rescheduling. Late payments may incur a 5% fee per 30 days overdue.

5. Revisions and Edits

5.1 Included Revisions

Each service package includes a specified number of revision rounds:

  • Commercial: 1-2 rounds
  • Narration: 2-3 rounds
  • Corporate: 2 rounds
  • Character: 2-3 rounds

5.2 Revision Requests

Revision requests must be submitted in writing via email. We aim to complete all revisions within 24-48 hours of request.

5.3 Additional Revisions

Additional revision rounds beyond those included in your package are available at $75 per 15-minute session or $50 per script change.

5.4 Scope Changes

Substantial changes to script content, character direction, or project scope may be treated as a new project and quoted accordingly.

6.2 Portfolio Rights

6.3 Client Content

Client warrants that they own all rights to scripts, logos, and other materials provided for production.

7. Usage Rights and Licensing

7.1 Standard License

All standard service packages include:

  • Unlimited usage within the specified project/media type
  • 1-year term for broadcast usage (renewable)
  • Worldwide territory

7.2 Extended Licensing

Buyout options for perpetual, unlimited, cross-media usage are available for an additional fee. Contact us for custom licensing agreements.

7.3 Exclusivity

Voices are non-exclusive unless an exclusivity agreement is purchased. We may use the same voice talent for competing projects unless exclusivity is secured.

8. Turnaround Time

8.1 Standard Turnaround

  • Commercial: 24 hours
  • Corporate: 24-48 hours
  • Narration: 48-72 hours
  • Character: 48 hours
  • IVR Systems: 24 hours

8.2 Rush Service

Rush service (12-hour turnaround) is available for an additional 50% fee, subject to talent availability.

8.3 Delays

We are not responsible for delays caused by client revision requests, late script delivery, or technical issues beyond our reasonable control.

9. Cancellation and Refunds

9.1 Project Cancellation

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before scheduled recording: Deposit is refundable minus a 10% processing fee.

Cancellations made within 48 hours of scheduled recording: Deposit is non-refundable but may be applied to a future session within 90 days.

9.2 Work in Progress

For partially completed projects, the client is responsible for payment for work completed up to the point of cancellation at the hourly rate of $150/hour.

9.3 Dissatisfaction

If you are not satisfied with the initial recording, we offer one complimentary re-record with a different voice talent. No refunds are issued for completed work that meets project specifications.

  • Secure rights to any third-party content provided
